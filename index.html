HOW IT WORKS

Three steps to
free profit

Arbitrage means both outcomes are covered. One always pays — you keep the gap.
01
We scan both platforms
Every 5 seconds we fetch live prices from Manifold and Polymarket. Our fuzzy matcher correctly pairs markets even when names differ between platforms.
02
We detect the gap
If YES on Platform A plus NO on Platform B totals less than 1.00 after both fees — that is arbitrage. You profit no matter what actually happens.
03
You act on it
The dashboard shows exact prices, profit percentage, and a live calculator — see the exact rupee amount before placing a single trade.

1
Fuzzy market matching
Platforms name the same event differently. fuse.js with 0.2 threshold correctly pairs mismatched market names across both platforms.
2
Fee-adjusted profit only
Both platform fees (~2–3% each) are subtracted before showing any profit. Every opportunity shown is genuinely profitable after all costs.
3
Both directions tested
YES(A)+NO(B) and NO(A)+YES(B) are both tested for every pair. Whichever combination profits gets surfaced — nothing is missed.
4
Near-miss tracking
Pairs almost at arb are shown separately so you can watch markets about to open — not just ones already profitable.
